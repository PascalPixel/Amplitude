Actionable analytics

Learn not only what your users are doing, but why they're doing it. Find out what you need to do to increase your conversion and engagement rates. Find out which groups of users have the most success with your app, across sources, dates, and devices. Use data to improve your app.

Mobile focused

We're 100% focused on mobile. All the data we display you is tailored to answer questions about app usage among your users. We automatically track users, sessions, devices, locations, and more on mobile to save you work.

Robust, realtime, scalable

We're always ensuring our code is compatible with every mobile device under every circumstance, no matter if a user is offline, has poor connectivity, or is on an older device. Our SDKs are open source so you can trust the code you're running. Our SDK still logs everything even if the user is offline- events are stored locally on the phone and uploaded when the user comes back online. Events show up within seconds on all Amplitude graphs. Works even with tens of millions of events a day.

Fast integration

After 5 minutes and one line of code, you'll be tracking daily, weekly, and monthly active users, sessions, retention, device types, platform, country, language, app version, location, and more. Add one line of code to track additional events with within a session. See how easy integration is for yourself.

Your data is yours

A lot of analytics companies try to keep you on their platform and make it hard to export data. We give you one click export to multiple data formats so you can always do a custom analysis without jumping through hoops.

See the big picture as well as the details

We display aggregated metrics for easy consumption so you know right away what's happening across your app. At the same time, we let you easily drill down to the history of a single user so you can figure out exactly how your app is being used on an individual basis.
